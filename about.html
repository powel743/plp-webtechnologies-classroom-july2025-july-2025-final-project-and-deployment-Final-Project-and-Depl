About RePET

RePET is a student-led initiative that turns PET bottles into usable polyester fiber. Our mission is to reduce plastic pollution while creating local economic opportunities and educating communities.

Vision & Mission

To lead in turning waste into high-quality material and to demonstrate sustainable, small-scale recycling solutions.

Team

Project lead: Collin — Industrial Chemistry student. Community coordinators and volunteers support collection and processing.
